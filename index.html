about us

what's make our coffee special!

First, it's the plant itself. Specialty coffee is exclusively made of a family of varieties under the Arabica species. Coffea Arabica is a delicate plant that is prone to diseases and requires specific conditions to grow- rich soil and high altitudes.

The coffees are assessed based on how they are grown, processed, their fragrance, body, aroma, acidity, and balance. If you brew a cup of specialty coffee, you'll be able to taste a drink that has multiple aromas and flavors.
